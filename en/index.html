01

Information Collection and Use

This privacy policy applies to the Pilot app for mobile devices and all related services operated by Francesco Lombardi (collectively, the "Application"). Francesco Lombardi is referred to herein as the "Service Provider".

The Application collects data when you download and use it. The categories of information collected include:

  • Identity and Authentication Data — Your email address and name, obtained through Sign in with Apple (Apple Inc.) or Firebase Authentication (Google LLC).
  • Professional Data — Your current profession and desired career goal, voluntarily entered during profile setup.
  • Roadmap Progress — Completion data for the career roadmaps generated by the app, required to personalize your paths and sync them across devices.
  • Technical Data — Your device's IP address, operating system, pages visited within the Application, date/time, and time spent.
ℹ️ Professional data (profession and career goal) is required for the service to function: it is used by the AI to generate personalized career roadmaps. Without it, some core features are unavailable.

Third-Party Access and Infrastructure

The Application relies on third-party providers for backend infrastructure and authentication management. Your personal data — including your saved career paths and roadmap progress — is synced to the cloud and associated with your account in order to ensure availability across multiple devices.

The main infrastructure providers are:

Firebase (Google LLC) — Backend & Authentication Sign in with Apple (Apple Inc.) — Authentication

Your roadmaps and career data are stored on the secure servers of these providers to enable multi-device sync. These providers process data solely for the stated purposes and do not use it for their own independent commercial purposes.

Note: Your data is never sold to third parties or used for advertising purposes.

The Service Provider may share your information in the following circumstances: to comply with legal obligations (e.g. in response to a court order); to protect its own rights or the safety of users; with trusted service providers acting on its behalf, who are contractually bound to comply with this policy.

Payments and Subscriptions

The Application offers in-app purchases and premium subscriptions. All transactions are handled directly by Apple via StoreKit — Apple's native framework for in-app purchases on iOS and macOS.

No financial data is collected by us: The Service Provider does not collect, process, or store any financial information, including credit card data, bank account details, or any other payment information. All monetary transactions occur exclusively through Apple's secure infrastructure.

For information on managing subscriptions, cancellations, or refunds, please refer to Apple's official instructions or your Apple ID settings. The Service Provider has no access to or visibility over your payment data.
